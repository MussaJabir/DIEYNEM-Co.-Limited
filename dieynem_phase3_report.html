Summary

Phase 3 is complete — the website and dashboard are now polished, fast and ready to prepare for launch. Where the earlier phases built the website and gave it a premium look, this phase focused on the finishing touches that make it feel professional, perform well, and stay easy and safe to manage.

For your visitors, the public site is now faster, smoother and easier for everyone to use — with instant project filtering, the ability to install it like an app, and the groundwork for a Swahili version. Your public website — what improved

  • Instant project filtering — visitors narrow your portfolio by service, sector, country and status, without the page reloading
  • Ongoing projects show animated progress bars that fill as visitors scroll — the site looks live and current
  • Faster loading — fonts and assets are served from your own site, with smart caching
  • The page no longer jumps or shifts while images load — a calmer, more professional feel
  • Usable by everyone — keyboard navigation, screen-reader support and strong colour contrast
  • Installable as an app on phones and computers, with a basic page even when offline
  • Swahili groundwork laid — a Swahili version can be switched on once the wording is translated

Your admin dashboard — what improved

  • A new enquiry pipeline overview with one-click export to a spreadsheet (CSV)
  • A dark mode for comfortable use in low light
  • A complete change history — see who edited what, and when
  • A recent-activity feed on the overview so nothing slips by
  • Optional two-factor authentication — each user can add a second sign-in step for extra security
