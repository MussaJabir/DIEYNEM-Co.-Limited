Who we are

One contractor, accountable end to end

DIEYNEM Co. Limited is a Dar es Salaam–based multi-disciplinary engineering contractor, incorporated in 2011 and registered with the Contractors Registration Board as a Specialist Contractor, Class One. Our work spans complete building services — electrical, ICT, fire-detection, air-conditioning and mechanical ventilation — alongside large-scale rural electrification and solar street-lighting infrastructure across Tanzania and Uganda.

We supply, install, test and commission complete electrical and electronic building systems — and we build the power infrastructure that energises them. One contractor, accountable from the 33kV switchgear to the last light fitting.
